What is Immersion Week

More than an event. A turning point.

Immersion Week is a deliberately designed experience for our global cohort — four days to step out of the day-to-day and step fully into one another. Through field experiences at NASA and Houston’s AI infrastructure, candid conversations with leaders from NVIDIA, HPE, and the energy sector, and unstructured time built for real connection — we go deeper into who we are as leaders and further into what we can build together.

This is our second time gathering as a cohort, and we won’t reconvene again until March 2027. The relationships we form in Houston will carry us through.

🧭
