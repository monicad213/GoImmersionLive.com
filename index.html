What is Immersion Live

More than an event. A turning point.

Immersion Live is a deliberately designed experience for our global cohort — four days of structured programming built around field experiences at NASA and Houston’s AI infrastructure, candid conversations with leaders from NVIDIA, HPE, and the energy sector, and unstructured time for real connection. We go deeper into who we are as leaders and further into what we can build together.

The program runs October 21–24. Many participants choose to arrive a day early or stay through the weekend to continue the conversation on their own terms.

This is our second time gathering as a cohort, and we won’t reconvene again until March 2027. The relationships we form in Houston will carry us through.
